Stranger Things is a science fiction show taking place in a small town in 1983 when middle schooler Will Byers is taken by a strange creature being hunted by the government. His best friends Mike, Dustin, and Lucas get themselves caught up in the scandal. As coverups lead to more coverups, a girl with crazy telepathic powers named Eleven appears, the monster resurfaces, and the only thing anyone can be sure of is that things are just about to get stranger.
